```longitudinal_jerk_costs(trajectory: TrajectorySample)```,
```orientation_offset_costs(trajectory: TrajectorySample)```,
```steering_angle_costs(trajectory: TrajectorySample)```,
```steering_velocity_costs(trajectory: TrajectorySample)```,
```distance_to_reference_path_costs(trajectory: TrajectorySample)```,
```path_length_costs(trajectory: TrajectorySample)```
